About

Hello! I am a public health systems analyst at Johnson County Public Health, where I try to create useful data and reports for people who are working on real problems in our community.

I completed my BA in informatics from the University of Iowa in 2024 where I enjoyed Iowa City so much I decided to stay. I enjoy working with and learning all I can on Python and ArcGIS products (and clearly web development) to use in my day to day, which consists of lots of data interpretation, creating community partnerships, survey development, and beyond.

Outside of work I'm interested in disc golf, running, and occasionally building on and starting small projects I find interesting, like this site.

There are no real expectations from having this website — it's mostly a place to keep track of and remember things I've worked on.
