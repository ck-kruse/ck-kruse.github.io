About

Hello! I am a public health systems analyst at the Johnson County Health Department, where I try to create useful data and reports for people who are working on real problems in our community.

I completed my BA in informatics from the University of Iowa in 2024 where I enjoyed Iowa City so much I decided to stay. I enjoy working with and learning all I can on Python and ArcGIS products (and clearly web developtment) to use in my day to day, wich consists of lots of data interpretation, creating community partnerships, survey development, and beyond.

Outside of work I'm interested in disc golf, running, and occasionally building on and starting small projects I find interesting, like this cite.

There are no real expectations from having this website — its mostly a place to keep track of and remember things I've worked on.

Johnson County Community Health Dashboard

ArcGIS Experience · Python · 2025

A dashboard and map that includes information from >450 local organizations so the public can find which best fit their needs and how to contact them. Info is sourced from internal databases along with data from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (or CMS NPPES for short). Also includes a SNAP map created by the County's GIS team (not me) in response to the government shutdown in late 2025.
